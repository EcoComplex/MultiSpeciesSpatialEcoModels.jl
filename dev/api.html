run_birthdeath!(landscape,λ,δ, steps )

MultiSpeciesSpatialEcoModels.step_birthdeath! — Method
step_birthdeath!(landscape, λ,δ)

Performs a step of the model a birth-death multispecies model, where landscape is the model grid, and λ and δ are vectors of the birth and death rate parameters. These vectors must have the same length that also defines the number of competing species. The length of λ and δ is the number of species.

The parameters λ and δ could change in any step of the model.
